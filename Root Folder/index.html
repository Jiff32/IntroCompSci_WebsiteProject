How to Make Paper Airplanes

Paper airplanes are a paper craft that involves folding paper in the shape of an airplane and throwing it in the air so it can fly. There
are many types of paper airplanes to make that come in all shapes and sizes. They are very fun to make, so we hope you enjoy this site and
the information it offers!

The list below contains various types of paper airplanes that you can build in your own home!
